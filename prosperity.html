Prosperity Olorunfemi is a Serial Entrepreneur and a Wealth Creation Expert who prides himself a Wealth Creation Gospellor whose Gospel is meant for the youth

He is obsessed with seeing young people build capacity and create wealth through value packed knowledge sharing and programmes.

In 2022, driven by strong desire to pave way for young people who are clueless and are in dire need of clarity to live a distinguished life, he founded STANDOUTCOMMUNITY, a community of over 300 young people a platform which dishes out relevant value for holistic development of young people to enable them navigate through the hurdles of life.

He is the first Nigerian Teenager to organize a TEDx Conference in Jibowu, few months ago.
