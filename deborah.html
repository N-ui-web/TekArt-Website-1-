Deborah Oludimu

Deborah Oludimu is the Team Lead at the A Pad for Her Initiative; an organization that was founded to provide a lasting solution to period poverty, and to bridge the gap between the average Nigerian female and proper menstrual health education.

She has reached out to over 6000 women and girls in Nigeria by providing them with period dignity and menstruation hygiene education.

In addition, she is an avid reader; a lover of African Literature, a writer and a Law Student passionate about tech and IP law.
